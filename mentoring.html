What do we do?

Machining

We use a mix of industrial powertools and handtools to construct our robot

Design & CAD

To design custom-sized parts we use Fusion 360 and Onshape to 3D print, CNC, and laser-cut parts

Programming

We use Java and Python to program our bots functionality and auto systems

Electrical

Focuses on wiring motors on the robot to more meticulous jobs: soldering, stripping wires, crimping on crimps, andersons etc.

Business

Manages team logistics and monetary expenses. During competition, business is responsible for presenting the Impact award.

Media

Responsible for creating and distributing content to the public. During competitions, the media team will take photos and videos of the event.
